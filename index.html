About BeirutPoly

BeirutPoly is a tribute to the resilience, creativity, and vibrant culture of Beirut. Experience the ups and downs of city life as you build your empire in this fun, chaotic board game.

Whether you're from Beirut, a visitor, or just curious, BeirutPoly brings you closer to the spirit of Lebanon's capital.
